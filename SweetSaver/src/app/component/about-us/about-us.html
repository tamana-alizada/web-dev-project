Our Mission

We make sweets smarter, not wasted. SweetSaver is on a mission to connect hungry customers with local bakeries and dessert shops that have surplus treats at the end of the day.

The Idea

Every day, bakeries and cafes are left with perfectly good, delicious pastries that haven't sold by closing time. At the same time, dessert lovers are looking for sweet deals. SweetSaver bridges this gap.

By reserving surplus treats through our platform, you save money, local shops recover their costs, and together we take a bite out of food waste.

Save Money

Enjoy premium, freshly baked goods at a fraction of the original price.

Support Local

Help your neighborhood bakeries thrive by purchasing their daily surplus.

Reduce Waste

Be an active participant in cutting down the environmental impact of food waste.
